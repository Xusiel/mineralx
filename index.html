Minerals

Quartz

Chemical Composition: SiO₂

Hardness: 7 (Mohs scale)

Color: Colorless, white, pink, purple

Uses: Used in electronics, watches, and jewelry.

Fluorite

Chemical Composition: CaF₂

Hardness: 4 (Mohs scale)

Color: Purple, green, yellow

Uses: Used in the production of fluorine and in jewelry.

Rocks

Basalt

Chemical Composition: SiO₂, Al₂O₃, FeO, MgO

Features: Dense, dark-colored, fine-grained

Uses: Construction, road-building, and as aggregates in concrete.

Granite

Chemical Composition: SiO₂, Al₂O₃, Fe₂O₃

Features: Coarse-grained, durable, and light-colored

Uses: Building material, countertops, monuments.
